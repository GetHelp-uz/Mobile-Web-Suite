ESCROW PAYMENT FLOW

Customer selects tool
Customer pays via Click / Payme / Paynet
Payment confirmed
Money stored in escrow

Worker scans QR
Rental starts

Customer returns tool
Worker scans QR again

System checks condition

If tool OK:

Escrow released
Shop receives payment
Platform receives commission
Customer receives deposit refund

If tool damaged:

Damage cost deducted
Remaining deposit refunded
